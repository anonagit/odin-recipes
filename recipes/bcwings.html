Buffalo Chicken Wings

Description

Everyone needs protein in their diet. One of the best sources of protein available is chicken. There are thousands of different recipees involving chicken but the best ones are the recipees that require little work and have the most rewarding taste; this is one of those recipees. Chicken wings and legs coated in buffalo sauce.

Ingredients

  • .5 lb of small checken wings/legs
  • 3 tbsp of olive oil
  • .5 tsp of salt
  • .5 tsp of pepper
  • .5 cup of homemade buffalo sauce
    • .5 cup of hot sauce
    • .5 cup cold unsalted butter
    • 1.5 tbsp of white vinegar
    • .25 tsp Worcestershire sauce
    • .25 tsp cayenne pepper
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 large ziplock bag

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 420 degrees
  2. Wash the chicken wings in cold water under the sink
  3. Toss chicken wings in a large bowl with salt, pepper, and olive Oil
  4. Place on an oven safe baking sheet at place in oven on middle rackle once properly heated
  5. Cook for 25 minutes
  6. Prepare the homemade buffalo sauce by mixing all the ingredients into a large bowl
  7. Pour sauce into a bottle or jar for easier storage
  8. Once chicken has finished cooking, remove from oven and carefully place wings into the large ziplock bag
  9. Pour buffalo sauce into ziplock bag with the chicken, zip the bag shut and begin to shake the bag until all of the wings are sufficiently coated in the sauce
