Chocolate Fudge Brownies

Description

Nothing says dessert like brownies. Hand sized, square, fluffy chocolatey goodness. Do yourself a favor and buy a premade mix. The time, effort, and money that goes into making this recipee from scratch is not worth it.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of chocolate fudge brownie mix
  • 3 Tablespoons Water
  • 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Stir Brownie Mix, water, oil and eggs in medium bowl until well blended
  3. Pour blended mix into a nonstick pan
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until toothpick inserted 2 inches from side of pan comes out almost clean
  5. Remove from oven and let brownies sit for 15 minutes to cool
