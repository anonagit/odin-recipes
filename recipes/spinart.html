Spinach Artichoke Dip

Description

Spinach artichoke dip, deliciously rich, perfectly creamy and cheesy, brimming with spinach and artichokes and itâ€™s always a crowd favorite. This dip is a must have for anyone hosting a party (weather it be for 10 or a party of 1)!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Parmasen cheese
  • 1 cup Sour cream
  • .5 cup of Mozzarella
  • 6 oz of Cream cheese
  • 16 oz of Artichoke
  • 16 oz of Frozen spinach

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Pour all of the ingredients into a large bowl
  3. Mix all of the ingredients until they are well blended. Use a batter mixer if you have one
  4. Pour all the contents from the bowl into an oven safe bowl large enough to contain it all. Use multiple oven safe bowls if you don't have a large enough one
  5. Place the bowl(s) into the oven on the middle rack and bake until the top layer is a golden brown (20-25 minutes)
